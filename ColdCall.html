Cold Call

Working with a team to participate in the Global Game Jam 2021 to create a game around the theme “Lost & Found”.


A game about reliance, "Cold Call" puts the player in the unique position of trying to help a lost researcher get to them. A different perspective on the story-rich genre, the game see's the player using their deduction skills and their watch tower tools to deduce where the researcher is in the abnormal forest. Direct them home and maybe learn a thing or two.
